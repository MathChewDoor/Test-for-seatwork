Key Guitar Features

Neck

The neck of the guitar is crucial for playability and tone.

Bridge

The bridge plays a significant role in the sound and intonation.

Fret

The frets divide the guitar neck into fixed segments to produce different pitches.

Body

The body of the guitar amplifies the sound and defines the guitar's shape and size.
